Marine Biology!!!

This website here will teach about many different aspects of marine biology.


Marine biology is the study of marine life. It studies their behaviors with the enviroments around them, and how they go on with life. This biology uses multiple different types of sciences that fit within itself. Scientists specialize in specific areas of the biology, whether it be indoor our outdoor.



In marine biology, There are multiple fields/branches of work you can do. Branches are just the more specific areas of work you can do. For example, some of those branches are,

  • Marine Biotechnology
  • Marine Ecology
  • Fisheries and aquaculture
  • Enviromentalist consultant
  • Dive operations management

Those are just a few types of specific areas you may work in. There are over 20+ more!
